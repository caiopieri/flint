# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in Flint a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our standards

**Encouraged:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the maintainers through [GitHub's private report flow](https://docs.github.com/en/communities/maintaining-your-safety-on-github/reporting-abuse-or-spam) or, for non-sensitive matters, by opening an issue. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially and appropriately.
